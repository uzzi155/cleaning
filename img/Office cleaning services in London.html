    Coronavirus Decontamination and Disinfection Cleaning Services for London Businesses

    12 Nov, 2020 •
    London is now in a second period of enforced lockdown that is due to end at the beginning of December. A second country-wide lockdown is in response to the continuing rise of daily confirmed cases and deaths from Coronavirus infections throughout England. To help businesses through this on-going crisis, the furlough scheme has been extended until the end of March 2021. As part of the revised scheme, anyone made redundant after 23rd September can be rehired and put back on furlough. For all businesses that are to remain open, the message from the UK Government is very clear. It is the responsibility of every business owner to keep premises free from spreading the Coronavirus. Coronavirus Decontamination of all shared premises is essential All businesses and organisations are to promote handwashing and cleanliness to make the workplace and communal areas COVID-secure. Employers must protect all employees and members of the public from risk. This includes taking precautionary measures to protect your workers and others from Coronavirus (COVID-19). Coronavirus can transfer from people to surfaces and be transmitted to others who touch the same surfaces. In an office environment, this includes handles, telephones, desktops, shared equipment, and kitchen appliances. Keeping your workplace clean and frequent handwashing will reduce the possibility of Coronavirus spreading and is a critical part of making and keeping your business ‘COVID-secure’. But to create the safest work environment for your employees and visitors, consider contacting a commercial office cleaning company providing Coronavirus Decontamination Services to deep clean and sanitise your office. As a cleaning services provider, Endersham has a key role in ensuring a range of preventive measures are implemented to reduce the impact of infection and to minimise the spread of the COVID-19 Coronavirus disease. Throughout the Coronavirus pandemic, we have continued to help manage the health and safety of London businesses and their employees. Our Coronavirus Decontamination and Disinfection Cleaning Services include: • Installing anti-bacterial gel and paper towel dispensers • Limiting use of hand dryers and/or supplementing them with paper towels and dispensers • Installing automatic water taps or flush devices • Introducing additional high-risk PPE waste sacks/bins • Supplying of social distancing partitions/signage • Reviewing current site specification and recommendation of service changes to ensure correct implementation of control measures For more information email or call us today on 0207 121 0020.
